Common Questions

Is this service really free?

Yes, 100% free. We are paid by the installers, not by you. You pay nothing and have no obligation to proceed with any quote.

Will I get sales calls?

Only from the installers you have agreed to hear from. If you tick the privacy option, installers will only reach you through us. Either way, you are in control.

Are the installers certified?

Yes — we only work with Clean Energy Council accredited installers. All installers in our network have been vetted for quality and reliability.

How accurate are the quotes?

Quotes are based on your address, roof type, and electricity usage. Uploading a recent bill helps installers give you a more accurate figure. Final pricing is confirmed during a site visit.

What if I rent?

Solar is currently only available to property owners. If you are renting, speak to your landlord — many are open to solar as it adds property value and reduces bills.
