Common Questions

Is the report really free?

Yes, 100% free. No credit card. No obligation to buy anything.

Will I get sales calls?

Only if you request a quote from an installer. We won't pass your details to anyone without your explicit permission in the report email.

How accurate is the savings estimate?

It's a personalised estimate based on your electricity usage, location, and typical solar output for your state. Actual savings depend on your roof and the system size you choose.

What if I rent?

Unfortunately solar is only available to homeowners. If you're renting, speak to your landlord — many are now open to solar as it increases property value.
